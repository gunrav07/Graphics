CERTIFIED PROGRAMME ON QUALITY CONTROL FOOD SAFETY, AND LABORATORY PRACTICES IN WHEAT BASED INDUSTRIES
28 November, 2016 to 20 January 2017

Upgrading skills in:

  • Quality Management System
  • Practical skills in operating testing laboratories on wheat based products
  • Understanding of Food Safety And Standards Act, 2006 and Regulations
  • Licensing and compliance of regulatory regime

Who should Attend:

  • Post Graduates and Graduates in Chemistry and Microbiology.
  • Post Graduates in Analytical Chemistry
  • Laboratory operatives who wish to enlarge knowledge base
  • Entrepreneurs, Managers and Supervisors in food businesses
  • Suppliers to bakery businesses
  • Technologists in Research and Product Development

Introduction to Programme:

This programme will be platform for skill development in food safety, standards, quality control and testing used in wheat based industry. Program will have emphasis on in depth study of all standards followed in bakery industry. Comprehensive and intensive program will match the industry requirements. The program will offer ready job opportunities Quality Management, Laboratory Management, and growth into factory management.

This Program has significance across total manufacturing system in bakery and involves following:

  • Selection of vendors and their competence to meet standards of materials used against specifications
  • Control of specification
  • Of ingredients, packaging material, and all other material to be used in operations
  • Process norms, control point checks and quality systems
  • Standards of finished goods
  • Pre acceptance testing of materials.
  • Sampling and testing of all ingredients to meet the FSSAI requirements and functional specifications for process.
  • Maintain records of all information needed  for
  • Control of product quality
  • Vendor identification for recall and regulatory requirements under FSSA
  • Assessment of vendors
  • Product improvement and new product development
  • Process control, on line checks, fault finding and corrective action
  • Assume responsibility for acceptance inward material and clearing finished product for market
  • Evaluate new materials and processes
  • Remain up to date with changes in regulatory system, global developments
  • Be core center for scientific and technology knowledge within the manufacturing system

Program has been designed to provide training inputs for persons already working in wheat based industry, course give a chance to understand science based quality management practices and equipped them growth in the industry.

Programs provides training inputs for entry level laboratory chemists and expose them to all elements of bakery controls which will help them to grow into knowledge based bakery scientists as they gain practical experience. Practical training will ensure that on successful completion, students can assume responsibility of an analytical chemist and can carry out all work required in a test laboratory for food operations and quality control management.
Duration: 8 weeks
