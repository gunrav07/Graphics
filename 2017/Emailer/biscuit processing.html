Biscuit Processing Course
April 21-23, 2017      Venue: AIBTM Campus      City/State: Greater Noida, NCR Delhi

This course is designed for:

Managers of retail biscuit bakeries
Managers of wholesale biscuit bakeries

Why should you attend?

Learn function of ingredients used for biscuits. Learn the stages of dough development and where you can use this knowledge to control quality. Learn how different mixing strategies affect the final product Learn control parameters of the biscuit process

Learn how to benchmark each critical step of the process. Learn to control quality and
minimize production cost at the same time. Learn an objective method to evaluate final product quality
