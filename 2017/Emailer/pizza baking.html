Science of Pizza Baking and Variety Pizzas
February 21-22, 2017        Venue: AIBTM Campus        City/State: Greater Noida, NCR Delhi
About Course:
In two days, you can learn the science behind the products. This will enable you to create new products based upon your customers’ needs as well as availability of ingredients. We will discuss how to create the type of pizza crust that you want. It will be intensive hands-on-training.

Agenda

Day 1 
Function of ingredients in pizza crust production
Pizza crust processing- control points and equipment used
Hands on Practice

Day 2
Troubleshooting pizza production
Variety Pizza Production – Hands on Practice
